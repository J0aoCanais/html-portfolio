Top 3 movies of this year

These are the three movies that I liked the most.

Titanic

This is my favourite movie of all time. The realism and the way the love of two people are shown is awesome.

Fast and Furious

I love cars, and this movie has a lot of special effects with explosions high-speed car scenes. These things made this movie one of my favourite.

Cars 3

This is my favorite animated film ever. How they made a film with cars that have love, team spirit, and competitiveness is pretty good for a car movie.
